About Us

Rubber and Enterprises is a leading rubber company based in kalugundi.We gather rubber from suppliers, sorting it according to quality, processing it accordingly, and then supplying it to customers based on their specific requirements. We have been providing high-quality products to our customers for over 18 years, with a strong commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.In addition to rubber, we also handle arecanut, cocoa beans, coconut, and pepper, although rubber remains our primary focus.

Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to delivering innovative solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients. We take pride in our eco-friendly practices and strive to minimize our environmental impact.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be a trusted partner in the rubber industry, providing superior products and services that exceed our customers' expectations.
